Frequently Asked Questions

What are empty Solana token accounts and why do they cost SOL?

When you receive SPL tokens on Solana, your wallet creates a token account that requires ~0.002 SOL in rent to stay open. When you sell or transfer those tokens out completely, the account stays open and continues charging rent. Rug Pull Refund finds these empty accounts and closes them, returning the rent SOL to your wallet. This is especially common after trading meme coins or receiving airdrops.

Is Rug Pull Refund safe to use with my Phantom or Solflare wallet?

Yes, Rug Pull Refund is completely safe! Your private keys and seed phrase never leave your wallet app. The app only creates transactions that you review and explicitly approve using your trusted Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Ultimate, Glow, etc.). We cannot access your funds, make transactions, or view your private keys without your permission. It's the same security model as using any DeFi app on Solana.

How much does Rug Pull Refund cost?

Rug Pull Refund charges a 7% service fee on recovered SOL only when recovery succeeds. For example, if you recover 0.01 SOL, the fee is 0.0007 SOL and you net 0.0093 SOL. If the scan finds zero empty accounts or recovery fails, you pay absolutely nothing. There are no subscription fees, hidden costs, or upfront charges.

Can this app recover my lost tokens or reverse rug pulls and scams?

No. Rug Pull Refund only recovers SOL rent from empty token accounts in your Solana wallet. It cannot reverse trades, recover stolen funds, undo rug pulls, or get back tokens you've sold. The name "Rug Pull Refund" refers to the rent recovery process, not scam reversal. Think of it as a wallet cleaning and optimization tool for the Solana blockchain.

Which Solana wallets work with Rug Pull Refund?

Any Solana wallet that supports the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter protocol works perfectly with Rug Pull Refund. This includes popular wallets like Phantom, Solflare, Ultimate, Glow, Backpack, and many others. If your wallet works with other Solana Mobile apps, it will work with Rug Pull Refund.

When will Rug Pull Refund be available on Google Play Store?

We're currently completing the Google Play review process and internal testing requirements. The app should be available on Google Play Store within 2-3 weeks. In the meantime, you can download the signed APK directly from this website - it's the exact same app that will appear on the Play Store.

How many empty token accounts can Rug Pull Refund close at once?

Rug Pull Refund can close all your empty token accounts in a single transaction, regardless of quantity. Whether you have 5 or 50 empty accounts cluttering your Solana wallet, one signature closes them all and recovers the SOL rent. This is much more efficient than closing them manually one by one.

Will this delete tokens I'm still holding in my Solana wallet?

No! Rug Pull Refund only closes accounts with exactly ZERO tokens. If an account has any balance (even 0.0001 tokens), it will not be touched. The app is specifically designed to safely clean up only the empty accounts that are wasting your SOL on rent fees.
