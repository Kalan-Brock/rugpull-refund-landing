Privacy Policy

Rug Pull Refund - Solana Wallet Cleaner

Last Updated: January 2025

TL;DR: Rug Pull Refund does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your wallet interactions stay on your device. We value your privacy above all else.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Rug Pull Refund ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application for recovering SOL rent from empty Solana token accounts.

Rug Pull Refund is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not operate backend servers to collect user data, and all wallet operations happen entirely on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Rug Pull Refund does not collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses)
  • Wallet private keys or seed phrases - These NEVER leave your wallet app
  • Wallet addresses or public keys - Processed locally only
  • Financial data (account balances, transaction amounts, token holdings)
  • Location data (GPS coordinates, IP addresses)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising IDs)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry (app usage patterns, feature interactions)
  • Cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics
  • Contacts, photos, audio, messages, or any other device data

Important: Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to sell, share, or compromise. Your privacy is 100% protected by design.

3. How the App Works (On-Device Only)

All operations in Rug Pull Refund happen entirely on your Android device:

  • Wallet Connection: You connect your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.) using the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter protocol. This connection is local to your device only.
  • Account Scanning: The app queries the Solana blockchain directly via public RPC endpoints to find empty token accounts associated with your wallet address. No intermediary servers are involved.
  • Transaction Creation: When you choose to recover SOL, the app creates a transaction locally on your device, which you then review and approve in your trusted wallet app.
  • Transaction Submission: The signed transaction is sent directly from your wallet to the Solana blockchain network. We do not intercept, store, or have access to this transaction data.

4. Third-Party Services

Solana RPC Endpoints

To query the Solana blockchain for your token accounts, the app connects to public Solana RPC endpoints (such as those operated by Solana Labs, Helius, QuickNode, or other providers). These services may log IP addresses and requests according to their own privacy policies, which are outside our control.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any RPC provider you choose to use:

  • Solana Foundation: https://solana.com/privacy-policy
  • Your selected RPC provider's privacy policy (if using custom endpoints)

Wallet Providers

When you connect your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Ultimate, Glow, etc.), that wallet app operates according to its own privacy policy. Rug Pull Refund does not have access to your wallet's internal data or private keys.

5. Data Security

Since Rug Pull Refund does not collect or store any user data on servers, there is no central database that could be breached or compromised. All security is handled by:

  • Your wallet app (Phantom, Solflare, etc.) which securely manages your private keys
  • The Solana blockchain which processes all transactions in a decentralized manner
  • Your Android device's security (screen lock, encryption, app permissions)

6. Permissions Required

Rug Pull Refund only requests the following Android permission:

  • INTERNET: Required to communicate with Solana RPC endpoints and the blockchain network. This is essential for scanning accounts and submitting transactions.

The app does NOT request permissions for camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any other sensitive device features.

7. Children's Privacy

Rug Pull Refund is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, please contact us so we can address the situation.

8. International Users

Rug Pull Refund can be used anywhere in the world where Solana blockchain access is permitted. Since no personal data is collected, there are no cross-border data transfers or GDPR/CCPA compliance concerns related to data storage. The Solana blockchain itself is decentralized and operates globally.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, or industry best practices. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Significant changes will be announced in the app or on our website
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

10. Your Rights

Since Rug Pull Refund does not collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, correct, delete, or export. You maintain full control over your wallet and blockchain interactions at all times.
